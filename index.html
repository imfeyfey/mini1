Services

Quality prints, every time!

Photo/Document Printing

Printing services that specialize in printing high-quality images and documents on various types of paper or other materials, such as canvas.

Laminate

A process of sealing a document or photo between two layers of plastic film to protect it from wear and tear, moisture, and other environmental factors.

Photo Sintra

A type of photo printing that involves printing high-quality images onto a durable, lightweight material called Sintra board, which is made of PVC plastic.

Photocopy

A process of reproducing multiple copies of a document or image by using a photocopier machine.

Scan

The process of converting a physical document into a digital format by using a scanner or a smartphone camera.

Stickers

The process of printing customized stickers on a variety of materials, such as vinyl, paper, or transparent film.

Business Cards

A small card that displays the contact information of an individual or a company, typically used for networking or promotional purposes.

Mug Printing

The process of printing customized images or text onto a coffee mug or other types of drinkware.

Invitations

A type of card that is designed to invite people to an event, such as a wedding, birthday party, or corporate event.

Ref Magnets

A type of promotional item that involves printing customized images or text on magnets that can be attached to a refrigerator or other metal surfaces.

PVC ID/Bag Tags

A type of personalized identification card or bag tag that is made of durable PVC plastic and can be customized with various designs, images, and text.

Tshirt Printing

The process of printing customized images or text onto a t-shirt or other types of apparel using various printing methods, such as screen printing, digital printing, or heat transfer.
